Initalize Conditions:

Rules:

This is a Zombie outbreak simulator. Specify the initial conditions - how long the simulation runs (number of generations, with each generation lasting one second), how many human agents to start with, and what proportion of those humans are Zombies - and then watch the game play out.

Agents have randomly generated personalities, and starting locations. They will then undertake actions each generation, based upon their surroundings and personality type. Some unlucky humans will be converted into Zombies and turn orange. Some unlucky Zombies will be destroyed by humans.

All agents have an energy level that will slowly decrease over generations. Humans have significantly more energy than zombies.
